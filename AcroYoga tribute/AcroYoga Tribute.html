What is AcroYoga?

“If it’s fun, it’s right!”

-Jason Nemer, founder of Acroyoga International.

Combining yoga flow, acrobatics, and circus arts, AcroYoga is one of the best expressions of the old joke “as much fun as you can have with your clothes on.” Are you looking to connect with your inner child, have tremendous amounts of silly fun, and build deep, trusting relationships with super-fit people? AcroYoga just might be for you.

The Origins

AcroYoga fatefully began in 2003 when the co-founders met at a party. Fortuitously, both were acrobats and had competed in partner acrobatics at a high level. The next week, they started forming the base of something that would grow into an international sensation. They certified their first group of teachers in 2006, and it’s been growing like bread dough in the tropics ever since.

Why I Love It

AcroYoga combines three of my favorite things: fitness, community building, and childishness. I’m an avid traveler, and one of the greatest gifts AcroYoga offers is an automatic community anywhere I go. Dropping into Lisbon? Look up the city’s Acro Facebook group. Headed to Timbuktu? Message a local Acro teacher. People are fanatics about this, and they love getting to know new humans who love it too!
